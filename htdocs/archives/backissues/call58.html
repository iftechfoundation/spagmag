Call for Submissions for SPAG Issue #58 (Deadline: May 21, 2010)

Hi, folks...

The time has come to start pulling together content for the next issue of SPAG (http://www.sparknet.com/spag), and I could use your help.

In particular, we have a whole lot of games to review from the Jay Is Games competition (http://jayisgames.com/cgdc7/) -- 30, to be exact. I'd like to put a team together to tackle these, as I've done with the big fall Comp the last couple of years. Therefore, if you are willing and able to review a handful of randomly assigned short, presumably casual text adventures, please let me know.

And Raising the Flag on Mount Yo Momma, by Juhana Leinonen, needs a review. Reviews of other games, old or new, are of course also welcome, as are articles, interviews, and films (that sort of thing being popular in IF circles these days). Drop me a line if you have a proposal to discuss.

The deadline for submissions is May 21, 2010.

Thanks in advance,
Jimmy
