Society for the Promotion of Adventure Games

SPAG is an informative e-zine designed primarily to keep the gaming public aware of textual interactive fiction and other types of interactive narrative available today. Most of the space is devoted to reviews. A new edition of SPAG is published each quarter. Generally, you can expect a new issue in January, April, July, and October of each year. If you would like to automatically receive each new issue of SPAG in your email box shortly before it appears on this site, feel free to subscribe.  The latest issue of SPAG is #46, published on October 17, 2006.

SPAG was founded by G. Kevin "Whizzard" Wilson, and is currently edited by Jimmy Maher. The SPAG website was designed by Felix Plesoianu.
